A1 Test 1 – Reading

Mark takes bus number 6 to go to work every morning. He usually sits near the window and reads the newspaper. The bus stop is close to his house.

1. What bus number does Mark take?

2. Where does Mark read the newspaper?

3. What kind of transport does he use?

A1 Test 2 – Reading

Joe is a plumber. He starts work at 8:00 and finishes at 12:30. He enjoys fixing pipes and helping people with water problems.

1. What is Joe’s job?

2. What time does he finish work?

A2 Test 1 – Reading

During the summer, our group goes to the beach every weekend. I am the leader and I plan the activities. Everyone helps clean up before leaving.

1. Where does the group go on weekends?

2. What is the writer’s role?

A2 Test 2 – Reading

My first job was at a restaurant. It was not easy because I had no experience. But my manager helped me learn and I became faster at my work.

1. What did the writer not have?

2. What did the manager help the writer to do?

B1 Test 1 – Reading

B1 Test 2 – Reading
